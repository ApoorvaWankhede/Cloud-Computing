Objectives

The European Union strives to support the physical health and well-being of its citizens. The aim of the Treasure Hunt for Health programme is to encourage an active lifestyle through participation in a transnational treasure hunt.

The Treasure Hunt for Health also provides a means for local businesses to expand their reach through partnership in the programme.
